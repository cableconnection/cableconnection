Privacy Policy

At Cable Connection, we are committed to safeguarding your personal data with transparency and integrity. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your data in accordance with industry standards and data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, address, phone, email when registering or contacting us.
  • Financial Information: Payment details used when you pay for services.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and device data.
  • Service Data: Account numbers, technical logs, and support interactions.

2. Why We Collect Information

  • Provide Services: Activating new services, processing orders, upgrades, and cancellations.
  • Customer Support: Contact and service data for resolving issues.
  • Billing and Payments: Process payments for services and execution charges ($49.99 to $199.99).
  • Improvement and Marketing: Using non-identifying data to improve services, marketing, and site traffic analysis.

3. Third-Party Service Providers

We partner with third-party vendors and certified service providers including payment processors and telecommunication companies. Please review their privacy policies for details.

4. Transparency in Pricing and Billing

  • Execution Fees: Clearly displayed on our website, ranging from $49.99 to $199.99.
  • Separate Billing: Third-party provider charges are outlined in agreements with those providers.
  • Subscription Fees: All recurring charges disclosed in advance.

5. Data Security

  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted in transit and storage.
  • Access Control: Only authorized employees have access.
  • Secure Payment Gateways: Industry-standard payment processors protect your transactions.

6. Your Rights and Control

  • Request to view, update, or correct data.
  • Request data deletion when services are no longer needed.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications anytime.
  • Request data portability in machine-readable format.

7. Compliance with Google’s Ad Policies

  • No deceptive tactics or false claims.
  • No misleading pricing or service representations.
  • No phishing or scamming practices.
  • No hidden or obscured terms of services or charges.

8. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

Cable Connection reserves the right to modify this policy. Updates will be posted on this page and communicated via email or notice on our site.
